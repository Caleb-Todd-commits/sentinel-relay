# ExampleCo Incident Policy

## Severity Levels

- **Low:** suspicious activity with no sensitive access.
- **Medium:** confirmed suspicious activity involving internal systems.
- **High:** confirmed token exposure, unauthorized access, or possible customer data access.
- **Critical:** confirmed data exfiltration, ongoing compromise, or regulated data exposure.

## Approval Requirements

The following require human approval:

1. Declaring confirmed breach.
2. Notifying customers.
3. Revoking production credentials.
4. Closing a high or critical incident.

## Required Report Sections

- Executive summary
- Timeline
- Evidence
- Systems affected
- Impact assessment
- Actions approved
- Remediation plan
- Follow-up tasks
